hideMouse

Hide the mouse pointer. Old mouseHide syntax is still valid but discouraged.

Syntax:
hideMouse

Example:
while not key(k_esc)
    printS("press 1 to hide mouse")
    waitKey(k_1)
    hideMouse
    printS("press 2 to hide mouse")
    waitKey(k_2)
    showMouse
wend

See also showMouse command.
