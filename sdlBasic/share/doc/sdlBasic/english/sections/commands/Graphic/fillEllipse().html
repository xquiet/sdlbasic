fillEllipse()

Draw a filled ellipse with current ink color at given coordinates with given radius couple. Radius must be positive numbers.

 Syntax:
fillEllipse( x , y , radiusx , radiusy )

Example:
for n = 1 to 100
    ink(rnd(16000000))
    fillEllipse( rnd(640), rnd(480), rnd(200), rnd(200) )
